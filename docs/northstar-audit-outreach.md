# Northstar Personalized Audit Outreach

## Format

Keep the audit light:

- one sentence on why the brand is a fit
- three observations from public signals
- one sentence on what Northstar changes
- one 15-minute CTA

Do not pretend to know their internal systems. Use public evidence, then inference.
