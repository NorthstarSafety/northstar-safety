# Northstar Partner Outreach

## Partner angle

Northstar is not legal advice and it is not a test lab.

It is the operating layer that sits between:

- the Shopify catalog
- the evidence packet
- official recall review
- the internal decision trail

That makes it useful to:

- compliance consultants
- child-product advisors
- QA operators
- Shopify agencies serving physical-product brands

## Easy ask

Use one of these asks, in order:

1. `If one client is already feeling this pain, I would love a short intro.`
2. `If you want to sanity-check the fit first, I can do a 15-minute partner walkthrough.`
3. `If a shared pilot is easier, I can keep the first scope to one store and 10 SKUs.`
