# Northstar Demo Checklist

## Before every call

- start Northstar locally
- confirm `http://127.0.0.1:8000` is loading
- verify the dashboard still shows:
  - 2 live products
  - 2 documents
  - 1 open case
- open these tabs in advance:
  - dashboard
  - products
  - Bicystar product
  - Bicystar alert
  - Bicystar case
  - SnackSprout product
  - settings

## Backup plan

If the app is slow or a page misbehaves:

- restart from `run_safety.py`
- rerun the smoke test
- if needed, narrate from the pre-opened pages instead of live clicking every screen

## Call flow

### 1. Set context

"This is a live single-store pilot for Shopify child-product brands. It is focused on the monthly workflow between your catalog, your evidence, and official safety signals."

### 2. Show the dashboard

Explain:

- what is currently in scope
- what evidence is still missing
- what cases are open

### 3. Show the products page

Explain:

- Northstar starts from the real Shopify catalog
- each product becomes a safety workspace

### 4. Show the Bicystar product

Explain:

- the live SKU
- the missing evidence checklist
- the CPC summary now linked
- the match history

### 5. Show the alert detail

Explain:

- one real confirmed overlap
- dismissed false positives
- written reasons that make the model reusable

### 6. Show the case detail

Explain:

- owner
- decision
- action summary
- investigation notes

### 7. Show the SnackSprout product

Explain:

- Northstar also creates value when there is no recall issue
- the evidence workflow is still useful on ordinary SKUs

### 8. Close on the pilot

Ask:

- would one store and your first 25 priority SKUs be enough to prove value?
- who would own this internally?
- how quickly could you provide the first evidence packet?

## After the call

- log objections immediately
- send the one-page and paid-pilot offer
- set a next step within 48 hours
