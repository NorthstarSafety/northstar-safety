# Northstar Partner Blurb

Northstar Safety is a Shopify-connected operating workspace for child-product brands. It helps small teams keep SKU-level product evidence organized, review official recall signals, and track investigations and actions in one place.

Northstar does not replace labs, legal advice, or agency work. It gives the merchant one practical operating layer between Shopify, the evidence packet, and the internal trail of what happened next.

The current founding pilot is intentionally narrow:

- one Shopify store
- first 10 priority SKUs
- 30 days
- $300 one-time

If you already work with child-product brands that struggle with scattered product documents, recall-review friction, or retailer-readiness follow-up, Northstar is designed to be an easy pilot rather than a heavy consulting engagement.
