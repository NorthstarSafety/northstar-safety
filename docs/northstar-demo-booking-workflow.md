# Northstar Demo Booking Workflow

## Goal

Move from reply to scheduled live walkthrough with as little friction as possible.

## Booking rule

Until a hosted scheduler is in place, book demos directly by email.

Offer two specific time windows in Eastern time and ask the prospect to choose one. Do not ask open-endedly for "a good time."

Use `founder@northstarsafetyapp.com` as the sender for all demo-booking emails.

## Standard booking reply

Use:

- one sentence on what they will see
- two concrete time options
- a promise to keep it to 15 minutes

## Recommended time blocks

Use founder-friendly windows that can be protected daily:

- Tuesday to Thursday
- 11:30 AM to 1:30 PM Eastern
- 4:00 PM to 5:30 PM Eastern

## What to send once a time is agreed

Reply with:

- confirmed day and time
- expected duration: 15 minutes
- video link if available, or note that you will send one shortly before the call
- one sentence on what the call covers
- sender signature from `founder@northstarsafetyapp.com`

## Demo format

For now, use screen share on the live local Northstar workspace unless a hosted environment is available.

Keep these tabs ready:

- dashboard
- products
- Bicystar product
- Bicystar alert
- Bicystar case
- SnackSprout product
- settings

## After the call

Within 30 minutes:

- update the pipeline stage
- log the main objection or buying signal
- send the one-page and paid-pilot offer
- if the answer is yes, send the live Stripe payment link immediately
- use Stripe invoice only if the buyer asks for invoicing
- ask for the next concrete step within 48 hours if they are not ready to pay on the call

After a successful pilot:

- send the live $149/month continuation link only after explicit agreement to continue
- use Stripe invoice only if the buyer wants monthly invoicing
