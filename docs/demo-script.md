# Northstar Demo Script

## Goal

Show that Northstar is already a live operating workflow for child-product brands that need control over evidence, official alert review, and internal action history.

## 12-minute flow

### 1. Start on the public site

Say:

"Northstar is intentionally narrow. It is built for child-product brands that need one place for Shopify SKUs, product evidence, official alert review, and the internal trail of what happened next."

Point out:

- the best-fit customer
- the founding pilot structure
- the low-friction 30-day scope
- the install path and contact path

### 2. Open the dashboard

Say:

"This is the operator view. The goal is to show what is missing, what needs review, and what needs action without making the team rebuild the workflow in spreadsheets."

Point out:

- synced products
- evidence coverage
- open alerts
- active cases
- pilot checklist

### 3. Open the products page

Say:

"Northstar starts from the real Shopify catalog. Every product becomes a safety workspace instead of just a title in the admin."

Point out:

- live products and variants
- readiness score
- evidence status
- active matches

### 4. Open a product detail page

Say:

"This is where normal product information and safety operations finally live together."

Point out:

- product profile fields
- evidence checklist
- uploaded documents
- related alerts
- related case trail

### 5. Open an alert detail page

Say:

"False positives are normal. The value is that review is structured instead of disappearing into email or Slack."

Point out:

- source notice
- confidence and rationale
- confirmed match
- dismissed matches with reasons

### 6. Open a case detail page

Say:

"Once a match matters, Northstar gives the team one place to keep the decision and action trail."

Point out:

- case status
- owner
- notes
- actions and timestamps

### 7. Open settings

Say:

"The pilot stays lightweight. One store, one owner, simple install state, and a clear next step if billing or hosting still needs founder help."

Point out:

- Shopify connection status
- billing state
- support and contact requests
- sync history

## Close

Finish with:

"The founding pilot is intentionally narrow: one store, first 10 priority SKUs, 30 days, and a clear decision point. If the workflow is real for your team, the next step is to connect the store, upload the first evidence packet, and prove the workflow on your live catalog."
