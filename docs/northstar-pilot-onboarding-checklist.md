# Northstar Pilot Onboarding Checklist

## Goal

Get a paid pilot from verbal yes to live kickoff with the least founder drag possible.

## Before payment

- confirm the internal owner
- confirm one Shopify store only
- confirm the first 10 priority SKUs
- confirm Northstar is an operations layer, not legal counsel
- confirm the pilot term and price:
  - `$300 one-time` for the 30-day founding pilot
  - continuation pricing discussed only if the pilot proves value

## Payment trigger

- invoice or payment link sent
- merchant replies with billing approval
- kickoff date tentatively held

## Before kickoff

- Shopify admin access or installed custom app with `read_products`
- store domain confirmed
- first evidence packet gathered for priority SKUs
- internal owner identified for weekly questions
- pilot success metric agreed

## Kickoff call agenda

1. confirm pilot scope
2. connect the Shopify store
3. select the first 10 priority SKUs
4. upload the first evidence packet
5. review the first alert and case workflow
6. agree the first 14-day success checkpoint

## First 7 days

- live sync confirmed
- evidence checklist visible on priority SKUs
- at least one document uploaded
- at least one alert decision reviewed
- first internal operator trained

## What to send immediately after close

- the paid-pilot offer
- kickoff time options
- Shopify access requirements
- evidence packet request
- short note on pilot success criteria
