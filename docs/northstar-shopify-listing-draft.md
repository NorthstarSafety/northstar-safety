# Northstar Shopify Listing Draft

## App name

Northstar Safety

## One-line summary

Track child-product evidence, official safety alerts, and investigation actions in one Shopify workflow.

## Short description

Northstar Safety helps Shopify merchants selling child products keep product evidence, official recall review, and internal action trails in one operating workspace.

## Detailed description

Northstar turns a scattered monthly safety workflow into one system:

- sync Shopify products and variants
- see missing or stale product evidence by SKU
- ingest official safety and recall signals
- review likely product-alert overlaps
- keep a written investigation and action trail

Northstar is designed for child-product operators who need a practical control tower, not another spreadsheet or consulting project.

## Primary merchant fit

- Shopify merchants selling child products
- operators managing 10 to 200 active SKUs
- teams that already have documents but lack a clean operating workflow

## Permissions explanation

Northstar currently requests product access so it can:

- sync products and variants
- attach evidence requirements to the right catalog items
- review official safety signals against the live catalog

Northstar does not require customer or order data for its current core workflow.
