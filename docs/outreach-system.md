# Northstar Outreach System

## Positioning

Use one sentence consistently:

**Turn scattered child-product evidence and manual recall review into one monthly Shopify workflow.**

## Sender rules

- send outreach, follow-ups, demos, and pilot discussions from `founder@northstarsafetyapp.com`
- use `support@northstarsafetyapp.com` for support, onboarding, and operational follow-through
- keep `hello@northstarsafetyapp.com` as the softer website-facing inbound alias when needed

## Target profile

Prioritize brands that look like:

- one Shopify store or one primary Shopify entity
- infant, toddler, feeding, nursery, or other child-product focus
- 10 to 150 active SKUs
- founder-led or lean ops / quality owner
- evidence exists somewhere but is scattered
- recalls, notices, or retailer requests are still reviewed manually

## Strong-fit signals

- product registration or recall pages already visible on their site
- mentions of testing, CPSIA, CPCs, food-contact declarations, or nursery safety
- lean team with obvious Shopify-first operating model
- customer-support or ops owner likely wearing quality or compliance responsibility too

## Weak-fit signals

- multi-brand or enterprise rollout needed now
- expectation of outsourced legal or compliance advice
- no meaningful product-evidence burden
- deep marketplace complexity beyond one Shopify-led catalog

## Pipeline stages

- `research_complete`
- `queued`
- `touch_1_sent`
- `touch_2_sent`
- `touch_3_sent`
- `replied`
- `demo_booked`
- `demo_complete`
- `pilot_discussion`
- `invoice_sent`
- `closed_won`
- `closed_lost`

## Daily founder block

1. Send 5 new first touches.
2. Send 5 follow-ups.
3. Update the pipeline immediately after each response.
4. Log one sentence on the main pain signal for every lead that replies.
5. Prepare the next demo using the live Northstar workspace.
6. Add objections and proven wording to founder notes.

## Outreach cadence

### Day 0

Send the first email if a named contact email exists. If not, use the website form.

### Day 3

Send a short bump with one observation about their child-product catalog, registration page, or evidence burden.

### Day 7

Send a stronger follow-up focused on missing-document visibility, official alert review, and internal action trail.

### Day 12

Use LinkedIn or the website form if email is still silent.

### Day 18

Send a polite break-up note and move on unless there is engagement.

## Discovery questions

- Who owns product evidence and safety review internally today?
- Where are certificates and supplier documents currently stored?
- How do you review official safety notices today?
- What creates the most drag right now: missing documents, recall review, or internal follow-through?
- What would make a 45-day pilot clearly worth paying for?

## Close motion

1. Show the live Northstar dashboard.
2. Open one product with missing evidence.
3. Show one alert with dismissed false positives.
4. Show one case with a written action trail.
5. Explain the founding pilot.
6. Ask for the first 25 SKUs and evidence packet.
