# Northstar Safety One-Page

## Northstar Safety

One Shopify-connected workspace for child-product evidence, recall review, and internal action tracking.

## What problem it solves

Most small product-safety workflows are fragmented:

- Shopify holds the catalog
- certificates and supplier documents live in inboxes or shared drives
- recalls are checked manually
- internal decisions are not written down in a reusable way

Northstar turns those steps into one monthly workflow.

## How it works

1. Sync the Shopify catalog.
2. Generate the evidence checklist each SKU should have.
3. Ingest official safety signals.
4. Review likely matches and dismiss false positives.
5. Keep the investigation and action trail attached to the affected SKU.

## Why merchants care

- faster monthly compliance review
- fewer missing-document surprises
- practical recall monitoring without spreadsheet hunting
- reusable internal audit trail when a product needs investigation

## What the live pilot already proves

- real Shopify SKUs can be synced
- evidence can be attached to live products
- official CPSC alerts can be matched to the live catalog
- true overlaps can be confirmed
- false positives can be dismissed with written reasons
- cases can be worked through an action log
