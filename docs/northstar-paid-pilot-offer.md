# Northstar Paid Pilot Offer

## What it is

Northstar Safety is a founder-led paid pilot for one Shopify store selling child products. It gives the merchant one place to:

- sync the Shopify catalog
- keep product evidence attached to the right SKU
- review official recall and safety signals
- log investigations, decisions, and actions

## Best-fit pilot customer

Northstar is the strongest fit for:

- US or US-plus-Canada Shopify merchants
- 10 to 150 active child-product SKUs
- lean founder, ops, sourcing, or quality teams
- brands with real evidence obligations but scattered files
- teams still checking recalls and safety notices manually

## Pilot term

Offer Northstar as a **45-day founding pilot**.

Why this works:

- long enough to prove a real monthly workflow
- short enough to feel low-risk
- simple enough to buy without enterprise procurement

## What is included

- one Shopify store connection
- one internal owner
- live catalog sync
- evidence checklist generation per product
- first compliance-profile pass on up to **25 priority SKUs**
- live CPSC ingest
- match review with false-positive handling
- case and action trail for confirmed overlaps
- founder-led kickoff session
- one follow-up working session during the pilot
- light async support by email during onboarding

All synced SKUs remain visible in the workspace. The active founding-pilot scope stays focused on the first 25 priority SKUs so the rollout stays fast and controlled.

## What setup is required from the merchant

- Shopify admin access or an installed custom app with product read access
- one internal owner for the workflow
- the first evidence packet for priority SKUs
- agreement that Northstar is an operating layer, not legal counsel

## Founding pilot pricing

Start with:

- **$750 flat** for the 45-day founding pilot
- **$249/month** after the pilot if the merchant continues on one live store

Why this structure works:

- easier for a small business owner to approve quickly
- enough revenue to keep onboarding founder-led and responsive
- clean decision point before the merchant moves into ongoing monthly use

## What is not included

- legal advice
- formal regulatory filings
- outsourced supplier chasing
- multi-store or multi-entity rollout
- custom retailer portal work

## Pilot success criteria

The pilot is successful when the merchant can:

- sync the live catalog
- see missing evidence by SKU
- review and record one real alert decision
- keep a clean action trail without founder intervention

## Pilot close line

"Northstar does not replace your legal counsel or lab. It gives your team one simple operating system for the monthly work between Shopify, product evidence, and official recall review."
