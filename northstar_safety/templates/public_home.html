How it works

One workflow from Shopify to written action trail

1Sync the store

Import products and variants from Shopify or stay in demo mode while the app is being configured.

2Fill the missing context

Add supplier, origin, age grade, and other details Shopify does not hold well enough for this job.

3Track proof by SKU

Keep certificates, lab reports, food-contact declarations, and other evidence attached to the right product workspace.

4Review official alerts

Refresh the signal feed, review matches, and write down false-positive logic so future review stays faster.

5Keep the action trail

For meaningful overlaps, assign an owner, capture the decision, and export a written summary when another party needs it.
